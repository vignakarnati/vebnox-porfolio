Frequently asked questions

Is this program really 100% free?+
Yes. There are absolutely no fees, no hidden conditions, and no charges at any point. Our mission is to remove barriers to digital education, not create new ones.
Do I need any prior experience or a degree?+
No. The Vebnox program is open to everyone, regardless of educational background or prior experience. We believe in learning by doing, and all you need is passion and a willingness to learn.
What kind of certificate will I get?+
You will receive a verifiable Certificate of Completion for each real-world project you successfully finish. This is far more valuable than a generic internship certificate as it's tied to a tangible outcome you can showcase.
As a startup, is it really free to post projects?+
Yes. Startups and mentors can post projects and get help from our intern community for free. In return, we ask that you provide constructive feedback and guidance to help our interns grow.
How much time commitment is required?+
The program is flexible. You can contribute based on your availability. We match you with projects that fit your schedule, from small tasks to larger, more involved projects.
Can I join as a core Vebnox member?+
Absolutely. We are always looking for passionate individuals to join our core team. Exceptional interns who demonstrate leadership and commitment will have the opportunity to become a more permanent part of the Vebnox mission.
